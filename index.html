About Me

I am a computational biologist specializing in integrative multi-omics analysis of cancer, particularly pediatric brain tumors. My work leverages deep learning, spatial transcriptomics, and DNA methylation to uncover malignant and immune cell heterogeneity. I am passionate about building explainable AI frameworks to transform precision oncology.

Research

  • Malignant and myeloid cell state prediction using H&E images
  • Multi-modal analysis of medulloblastoma subgroups
  • Deep learning models (ViT, GNN, MLP) for histopathological inference
  • Epigenetic regulation and spatial omics in tumor progression
